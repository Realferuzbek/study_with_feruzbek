# Ask AI

Ask AI answers questions about StudyMate features and how to use them.

What it can do:
- Explain what features are on the site and where to find them.
- Share public updates like todays mantra, live sessions, and leaderboard top.

What it cannot do:
- It will not answer unrelated general knowledge questions.
- It will not reveal admin or internal system details.
- It does not expose private account data to guests.

Privacy:
- Personal answers require a signed-in account.
- The assistant only uses site-specific information to reply.
